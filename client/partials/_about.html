A little bit about me..

I have a Bachelors Degree in Psychology from San Diego Christian College, a Masters Degree in Christian Counseling from Trinity Theological Seminary and am currently concluding the certification process through the Association of Certified Biblical Counselors.

I have been married for 36 years, was a stay at home mom while homeschooling our 4 children for 20 years, taught various women’s Bible studies, mentored women and taught various workshops at women’s retreats. Amidst life we also fostered 2 kids. Since 2002 it has been a passion of my heart to lead annual short term mission trips to Honduras. If it’s July and you can’t track me down, that’s because I’m in Honduras!
